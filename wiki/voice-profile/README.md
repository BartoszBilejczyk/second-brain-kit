# voice-profile/

This folder holds pages that define **how you communicate** — your tone, rhythm, patterns, recurring phrases, what you never say, how your style shifts by platform.

`brain-ingest` writes these pages as it learns your voice from transcripts. Do not create pages here manually — let the AI build the profile from your actual recordings.

`brain-query` reads every page in this folder before generating any content in your voice. An empty `voice-profile/` means generic AI output. A populated one means output that sounds like you.

---

**What ends up here (examples):**
- How you open a piece of content (direct statement vs. question vs. story)
- Words and phrases you use repeatedly without noticing
- Your relationship with vulnerability — what you share and what you don't
- How your tone shifts between languages (if bilingual — e.g. Spanish and English)
- What you never say — clichés, hedges, corporate-speak you avoid

---

The more recordings you ingest, the richer this folder gets.
